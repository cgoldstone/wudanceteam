Washington University Dance Team



Washington University Dance Team is the official dance team of the Wash U Bears! We perform jazz and hip hop dance routines during half time of home football and basketball games. We also perform at various events on campus throughout the year, such as Dance Marathon, and Relay for Life. Our choreographer, Ashley Tate, is the owner of AshleyLiane Dance Company, located in St. Louis. She has extensive experience in formal dance training and choreography.
